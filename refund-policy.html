Refund & Cancellation Policy

Clear guidelines for payments, cancellations, and refunds at Emergexians.

1. Introduction

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which payments made to Emergexians for website development, mobile app development, software solutions, digital services, and other offerings may be refunded or canceled.

2. No Refund Policy (Primary Clause)

All payments made to Emergexians are generally non-refundable once the project has been initiated, resources have been allocated, or work has started. This is because our services involve time, effort, and technical resource investment.

3. Cancellation Policy

Clients may request cancellation before the project begins. If cancellation is requested after the project has started, only the remaining unused portion of the work (if applicable) may be considered. Emergexians reserves the right to decide cancellation terms based on project stage.

4. Partial Refund (If Applicable)

In rare cases, partial refunds may be considered only if no substantial work has been completed. Any refund amount will be calculated after deducting costs for work completed, resources used, and administrative expenses.

5. Advance Payments

Advance payments are non-refundable as they secure project slot, planning, and initial execution.

6. Delay or Non-Delivery

Emergexians is not responsible for delays caused by incomplete client requirements, delayed approvals, third-party issues, or unforeseen technical challenges. Such cases do not qualify for refunds.

7. Third-Party Services

Payments made for third-party services such as domain registration, hosting, APIs, plugins, or integrations are non-refundable as they are governed by third-party providers.

8. Payment Disputes

Clients agree not to raise unjustified disputes or chargebacks once the project has started. In case of disputes, both parties will attempt resolution through mutual discussion.

9. Policy Updates

Emergexians reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the updated terms.

10. Contact Information

For any questions regarding refunds or cancellations, please contact Emergexians through our official website.
