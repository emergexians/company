How Emergexians Designed a Subscription-Based NGO Platform for Donations, Campaigns & Recurring Giving

A custom SaaS platform built to help NGOs create campaigns, collect online donations, manage subscriptions, and streamline digital fundraising through a modern and scalable system.

Project Overview

The platform was created to help NGOs digitize fundraising operations, launch donation campaigns, manage supporters, and scale recurring contribution models through a single web-based ecosystem.

Industry Focus

Social impact organizations, NGOs, fundraising teams, and nonprofit campaigns requiring digital donation and supporter management infrastructure.

Business Challenge

Many NGOs lack modern digital systems for campaign publishing, monthly giving, donation collection, and centralized administrative control.

Emergexians Solution

A scalable NGO SaaS platform with pricing plans, campaign management, donation flow, onboarding, admin dashboard, and payment integration.

Problem Statement

NGOs often struggle with fragmented donation operations and limited technical infrastructure, which affects fundraising scale, donor retention, and operational transparency.

  • No centralized digital fundraising system
  • Difficulty managing campaigns and donor data
  • Lack of recurring donation automation
  • Manual onboarding and administrative overhead
  • No structured SaaS model for NGO subscriptions

Solution Delivered

Emergexians designed a platform that combines fundraising, subscriptions, onboarding, public campaign visibility, and admin-level governance into one product.

  • NGO registration and subscription plans
  • Campaign creation and public donation pages
  • Recurring donation-ready architecture
  • Admin control for plans, NGOs, and approvals
  • Payment flow integration for digital collections

Core Features Implemented

The product was structured like a real SaaS platform with subscription logic, donor-facing pages, NGO dashboards, and platform administration.

Subscription Plans

Multi-plan structure for NGOs with feature-based pricing and platform access control.

NGO Onboarding

Dedicated registration workflow for organizations after successful plan purchase.

Campaign Management

Create and manage fundraising campaigns with public visibility and donor access.

Recurring Donations

Architecture designed to support monthly giving and recurring donor contribution flows.

Payment Integration

Razorpay-ready donation and subscription payment flow for platform monetization.

Admin Governance

Super admin panel for approvals, plan management, NGO control, and platform visibility.

Execution Approach

The platform was approached as a scalable product, not just a website, with separate user journeys for admins, NGOs, and donors.

Step 01
Product Strategy & Flow Mapping

Defined subscription flow, NGO onboarding process, donor journey, public campaign pages, and platform administration.

Step 02
SaaS Architecture Planning

Structured user roles, plan logic, campaign modules, donation handling, and administrative governance layer.

Step 03
UI & Dashboard Development

Designed responsive interfaces for NGOs, donors, and admins with clarity around subscription, campaigns, and actions.

Step 04
Payment & Platform Readiness

Integrated monetization-ready flow and prepared the system for live onboarding, plan purchase, and donation processing.

Business Impact

This platform created a foundation for NGOs to digitize fundraising and for the business to operate a recurring-revenue SaaS product.
