Project Overview

A real estate business needed a centralized solution to manage inquiries, allocate leads, monitor sales performance, and reduce lead leakage caused by manual processes.

Client Industry

Real estate business handling property leads, sales coordination, team assignments, and customer follow-up processes.

Core Challenge

Leads were managed across Excel files, phone calls, and WhatsApp, causing delays, poor tracking, and missed conversion opportunities.

Emergexians Solution

A custom Real Estate CRM with lead import/export, assignment logic, admin dashboard, filtering, and workflow automation.

Problem Statement

The client’s internal operations were dependent on disconnected tools and manual handling. This created inefficiency at multiple levels of the sales pipeline.

  • Scattered leads across multiple channels
  • No structured follow-up mechanism
  • Manual lead allocation to sales team
  • Limited visibility into conversion status
  • Frequent lead leakage and delayed action

Solution Delivered

Emergexians built a business-focused CRM platform tailored to the client’s exact process flow, enabling better control, faster response times, and improved team accountability.

  • Centralized lead dashboard
  • Round-robin assignment logic
  • Lead import/export via Excel
  • Status-based tracking and filtering
  • Responsive admin interface for easy usage

Core Features Implemented

Every feature was designed to solve a real business bottleneck and create an efficient sales operations system.

Lead Management Dashboard

Unified admin panel to manage, review, and monitor all lead records from one place.

Import / Export Leads

Bulk lead upload and export functionality to support operational flexibility and reporting.

Round-Robin Assignment

Automated distribution of leads to available team members for balanced workload allocation.

Advanced Filtering

Filter leads by date, status, and assigned members to accelerate day-to-day decision-making.

Inline Lead Editing

Fast editing via modal-based UI for streamlined updates without leaving the workflow screen.

Team & Role Management

Assign and manage members efficiently while maintaining operational clarity and accountability.

Execution Approach

The CRM was developed with a business-first mindset, focusing on usability, performance, and process optimization.

Step 01
Requirement Discovery

Mapped the client’s real estate sales workflow, lead touchpoints, and operational gaps to define platform requirements.

Step 02
CRM Architecture Planning

Structured the system for lead capture, role handling, assignment logic, and reporting-oriented data management.

Step 03
UI & Workflow Development

Created a responsive dashboard with fast-access controls, modal interactions, and user-friendly navigation.

Step 04
Optimization & Deployment Readiness

Refined data flow, improved lead handling efficiency, and prepared the system for ongoing business usage and scaling.

Business Impact

The CRM helped transform lead operations from fragmented manual handling into a centralized, measurable, and performance-oriented workflow.
